CureTalks Webinar: Climbing Mount Kilimanjaro for the MMRF with Myeloma Patient Advocate Matt Goldman 

with Matt Goldman
September 26th,2017.6PM EST
In February 2017, along with 5 other patients and 10 individuals who have had their lives impacted with myeloma, Matt Goldman climbed Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania, Africa. They were raising funds for Multiple Myeloma Research foundation (MMRF), which is a leading organization working to find  myeloma cure. Kilimanjaro is 19,000 feet high. The journey took 8 days where they covered over 40 miles. Matt says " I feel quite fortunate. Reaching the peak was a life changing and life defining moment." Join us to hear Matt relive his experience.

Multiple Myeloma: Minimal Residual Disease Testing as an End Point Treatment
with Dr. Ola Landgren
August 31st, 2017. 6PM EST Listen
Most myeloma patients, even those in complete response may show signs of relapse at some point of time. So testing and quantifying Minimal Residual Disease (MRD) has become essential to obtain better prognostics correlations. Researchers believe that MRD testing could help them compare myeloma therapy strategies, evaluate new treatment faster, and guide treatment decisions. The myeloma panelists are talking to Dr. Ola Landgren, a lead researcher in the field, on what MRD testing could mean to your treatment, and about how MRD testing could become a new and earlier end-point in myeloma treatment.
